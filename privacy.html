Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sofia ("the App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our content scheduling and publishing tool for TikTok and other social media platforms.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: basic profile information (username, display name, profile picture) provided by TikTok when you authorize the App via OAuth.
  • Access tokens: TikTok access and refresh tokens used solely to publish content on your behalf, at your explicit request.
  • Content data: video files, captions, thumbnails, and scheduling metadata that you upload or create within the App for the purpose of publishing.
  • Technical data: device and log information needed to operate and troubleshoot the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your TikTok account and publish or schedule content that you explicitly choose to upload.
  • To operate core features such as scheduling, content management, and delivery status reporting.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App.

We do not sell your personal information or your content to third parties.

3. How We Share Information

We only share data with TikTok's API to perform the actions you request (such as publishing a scheduled video). We do not share your access tokens or content with any other third party, except where required by law.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and account credentials are stored in encrypted form. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account connection and content data only for as long as necessary to provide the App's services, or until you disconnect your TikTok account or request deletion.

6. Your Rights

  • You may disconnect your TikTok account from the App at any time.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You may revoke the App's access to your TikTok account directly from your TikTok account settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
