Sofia

A content scheduling tool that lets creators and page managers plan and publish videos to their own authorized TikTok accounts.

What Sofia does

Sofia helps creators and page managers organize, schedule, and publish video content to TikTok in a structured, reliable way — connecting securely to your own TikTok account through official login and authorization.

Scheduling Plan your video publishing calendar in advance.
Secure Login Connect your TikTok account via official OAuth authorization.
Content Management Organize titles, captions, and media in one place.
Publishing Status Track whether your content was published successfully.

Who it's for

Sofia is built for individual creators and page managers who want a simple, organized way to manage their TikTok publishing workflow without manually uploading videos one by one.
